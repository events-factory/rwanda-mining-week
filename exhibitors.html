Exhibitors

Meet Our Exhibitors

Rwanda Mining Week brings together leading companies and organizations from across the mining value chain. Our exhibition floor features the latest technologies, services, and innovations driving the future of sustainable mining in Africa.

Connect with industry leaders, discover cutting-edge solutions, and explore potential partnerships during the three-day exhibition.

Exhibition Categories

Mining Equipment & Technology

Cutting-edge machinery, tools, and technological solutions for efficient and sustainable mining operations.

Mineral Processing

Advanced processing technologies, equipment, and services for mineral extraction and refinement.

Sustainable Mining

Environmentally friendly solutions, renewable energy applications, and sustainable practices for mining operations.

Safety & Security

Equipment, training programs, and technologies designed to ensure worker safety and site security.

Financial & Investment Services

Financial institutions, investment firms, and services specialized in mining sector financing.

Consultancy & Support Services

Professional services including legal, consulting, logistics, and other support services for the mining industry.
